My Project Idea

Inspired by other apps and website my idea is a smartphone application which functions as checklist/tracker for any video games you are playing and which ones you have completed or have yet to complete.
I have 2 versions of this app in my mind, an ideal one which would require a large amount of work as well as integration's with other existing products and services as well as an online database which would sync
your profile across multiple devices and allow you to follow your friends and see their progress. And a simplified, more realistic version which would function more simply as just an offline checklist for individual users.

There is a similar app that I use for TV shows and Movies (TV Time) which allows you track the TV shows that you are watching, which episodes you’ve seen and which you are yet to see, it notifies you when new episodes are
available, as well as allowing you follow your friends and see what they are watching. This is a fantastic app however I find myself wishing that I also had one that allows me to track the video games that I play as well,
I have seen several of these but they never have all the functionality that I want. So I’d like to design my ideal version of such an application.

The version of my app that I’ll describe is my ideal version but I understand that many of these features may not be feasible due to my knowledge level or due to requiring integration with other large existing services.
To begin with my application would require a database of video games which could searched, most probably using a service such as IGDB (Internet Games Database www.igdb.com) to create the list as a start point. Afterwards it would
allow users to add games to their profile as either games they have completed, games they are currently playing, games that are in their backlog, or games that would like to acquire in the future (a wishlist). Ideally I would also to
add game specific options such as things like what level/rank they are, which guild and server they play with/on or as well as achievement/trophy completion, and possibly also an LFG (Looking For Group) system to allow players
to find other players to play with. I would also ideally like an automatic system where you could link your account with other services such Steam, Xbox Live, PSN, and Nintendo to automatically add any games you have played to your
profile, as well as any achievements/trophies you have unlocked, and how long you have played each game. I would also like a “friend” system where you could add your friends and be able to see their profiles to see which games they
have finished, which they are currently playing, and which games they have on their wishlist. Perhaps having integration with the other platforms mentioned earlier to allow you to add any friends who have also linked these accounts.
It could also act as a social hub allowing to view which of your friend from any of the linked platforms is online and what they are playing at that time. Adding a price-tracker it could also keep track of the prices of games on the
digital storefronts and notify you when any games on your wishlist have gone on sale, as well as a page which shows which storefronts are having a sale and everything that is on sale. It could also link to sites such as
TrueAchivements.com and TrueTrophies.com, and provide guides for ingame accomplishments and other ingame tasks. As well as possible links
to sites such as howlongtobeat.com, Systemrequirementslab.com and speedrun.com to provide additional information about games. The simplified, more realistic version of my app would obviously strip back a lot of the more ambitious
features and just keep the games checklist, the game status (Completed, playing, backlog, want) and hopefully the online profile and friend functionality.

Most obviously it would require a PC and an IDE to write to code for such a project, as well as a smartphone to test and run the application. Additionally it would require several different existing API’s including the IGDB,
Xbox Live, PSN, Steam and a price tracker. I am sure that it would require many more things that I am unaware of at this moment.

It would require a decent understanding of one of several different coding languages and the proficiency in using an IDE to write said code in order to create the framework for the application. It would would also require the
ability to acquire and integrate several different API’s into the app for the outside resources required for the more ambitious aspects. I believe a simplified version of this app could be quite feasible to acquire all the skills
and software to create as what is required for the primary function should be reasonably attainable; the IGDE is available for anyone to use though it may cost. However my more ideal version is much less feasible as it requires
cooperation from several large existing companies and is unlikely to be available for anyone to be able to use.

This app would be a great tool for gamers to use to keep track of which games they have played, which they are currently playing and which they still need to complete. Ideally this app would be a “one-stop-shop” for most video
information that you would need and become an important companion to gamers around the world.
